Building the online presence for a local institution

For 30 years, Bert Gallery has been a treasured part of the Rhode Island gallery scene, thanks to it's niche as the best purveyor of historic Rhode Island artworks. Bert Gallery's patrons are savvy, well informed and generally know what they want as soon as they arrive at the gallery, thanks to it's extensive online presence. BertGallery.com has come to serve as the first interaction between patron and gallery, and a treasure trove of historic information about Rhode Island art, thanks to it's extensive online archives. Recently rebuilt from the ground up for mobile, tablet and desktop, the new Bert Gallery looks to serve all art patrons, whatever they're looking for, and on any device they choose.
